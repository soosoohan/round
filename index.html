The game starts with all vowels revealed.
Look at the vowels and guess which consonants are included, then click the consonant buttons.
Correct consonants will appear in their positions, and you lose 1 point for each wrong consonant.
Each round is worth 10 points, and the final score is calculated out of 100 points over 10 rounds.
Game files are arranged by difficulty level, getting harder as you go down.
